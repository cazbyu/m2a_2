Support the Cause

Your donation supports African entrepreneurs and local high school students. The suggested donation is $15, but we gratefully accept any donated amount.

Your donation will be added to your entrepreneur boost cart. Payments processed securely via Stripe. All funds go to the Sandy Rotary Foundation, a 501(c)(3) organization.

🏴 Rotary Shark Pond

Where young leaders meet global entrepreneurs

🎓

The Sharks

A DECA business club in Sandy, Utah will serve as the "Sharks" — evaluating business plans, learning about the countries, and selecting the winning entrepreneurs. Two finalists will pitch their ideas live to the club!

🌎

The Entrepreneurs

Twelve entrepreneurs from Zambia, Malawi, Lesotho, Kenya, and Uganda are competing for an opportunity to fund their business with a 0% interest microloan. Their selection is tied to your bracket performance — but you can give them a Boost to push them forward!

🏆

Everyone Wins

Rotary provides capital funding for the entrepreneurs and a small scholarship for the club. When your team wins, you help local and global entrepreneurs win.

🚀 Give your favorite entrepreneur a Boost! Your boost is your vote — the entrepreneur with the most support advances.

If you want to sponsor a Rotary Shark Pond service project for your club, school, or organization, contact Paul Cazier with the Sandy Rotary Club at 703-981-2213 or email at paul.s.cazier@gmail.com.

Meet the Entrepreneurs

Each tournament team is randomly assigned to an entrepreneur. When your team wins, that entrepreneur earns points. Give them an extra Boost to push them forward!
